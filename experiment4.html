Shivalik College of Engineering

A Premier Technical Institution in the Foothills of Dehradun, Uttarakhand

Legacy Since 2008

Established in 2008 under the Rijan Educational Society, Shivalik College has grown into a beacon of technical excellence in North India. Over the past 16 years, we have transitioned from a focused engineering center to an autonomous powerhouse recognized by the UGC.

Our history is defined by a 92% placement record and a commitment to producing industry-ready professionals for the global market.

Our Core Specializations

Computer Science (CSE)

Specializations in AI, Machine Learning, and Data Science in partnership with industry leaders.

Mechanical Engineering

Focus on robotics, smart manufacturing, and digital manufacturing workflows.

Electrical Engineering

Advanced studies in IoT-integrated power systems and semiconductor design.

Civil Engineering

Modern structural engineering and sustainable infrastructure design.

Lush Green Dehradun Campus

Located on the Shimla Bypass Road, our 50-acre campus offers a serene environment conducive to high-level research and learning.

The campus features smart classrooms, high-speed Wi-Fi, departmental libraries, and the unique "Learning Resource Center" dedicated to the former President of India.
